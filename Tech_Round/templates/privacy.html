Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sakshatakr, accessible at https://sakshatakr.com. Protecting the privacy of our visitors is one of our top priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information collected by Sakshatakr and explains how we use and protect this information.

Information We Collect

When you register on our website, we may collect personal information, including your name, email address, and other details necessary for providing our interview preparation services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To deliver personalized interview preparation services.
  • To enhance and improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  • To communicate with you, including providing customer support and sharing important updates.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

Cookies

Sakshatakr uses cookies to store visitors' preferences and to customize website content based on browser types and other information. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

This Privacy Policy applies only to Sakshatakr. We recommend reviewing the Privacy Policies of any third-party websites or advertisers for more information on their data practices.

Your Data Protection Rights

We want to ensure you are fully aware of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure: Under certain conditions, you can request the deletion of your personal data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sakshatakr.com.
