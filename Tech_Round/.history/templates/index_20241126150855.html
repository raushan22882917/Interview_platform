FAQ

Explore our frequently asked questions to help you better understand our processes and services.

What types of questions are asked in the HR round?

The HR round typically includes questions about your background, strengths and weaknesses, and your motivation for applying to the company.

How should I prepare for the HR interview?

Research the company culture, practice common HR questions, and prepare to discuss your resume in detail.

What is the purpose of the HR round?

The HR round assesses your fit within the company's culture and evaluates your soft skills and communication abilities.

How long does the HR interview last?

HR interviews usually last between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the company and the number of questions asked.

What topics should I focus on for the tech round?

Focus on your core programming languages, algorithms, data structures, and system design concepts relevant to the job description.

Are coding challenges part of the tech interview?

Yes, many tech interviews include coding challenges to assess your problem-solving and coding skills.

How can I improve my chances of success in the tech round?

Practice coding problems on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank, and participate in mock interviews to enhance your skills.

What is the format of the technical interview?

The format may include live coding sessions, whiteboard challenges, or take-home assignments, depending on the company.
