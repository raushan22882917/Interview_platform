Interview Process

  • 1. Enter the Target Company Name: Type in the company name in the provided input field and click "Start Interview" to begin.
  • 2. Start the Interview: Click on the “Start Interview” button to begin answering questions tailored to the company.
  • 3. Question Display: AI will display and read aloud each question, guiding you throughout the process.
  • 4. Speech Recognition: The system will listen for your spoken response. Speak clearly for the AI to recognize your answer.
  • 5. Answer Submission: You can either speak your answer or type it in the text box and click "Submit".
  • 6. Feedback and Scoring: The AI provides feedback and scores based on your answer quality, e.g., “Excellent,” “Good,” etc.
  • 7. Next Question: The next question will appear automatically, and the interview continues until all questions are completed.
